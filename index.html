PB&J

Let's learn how to make a Peanut butter and Jelly sandwich!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly
  • 2 Slices of Bread

Instructions

  1. Lay out 2 slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice.
  3. Spread jam/jelly on the other slice.
  4. Stick the two slices togther.
  5. If you want to cut it into smaller portions, do so carefully.
  6. You can also cut the crust off if you so choose.
  7. Enjoy!
